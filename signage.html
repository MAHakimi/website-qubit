QBSign

Integrated digital signage solution based on professional B2B smart signage display

Digitally Engage With Clients using QBSign

QBSign is a comprehensive advertising management system designed for the entertainment and hospitality industry. Our web-based platform enables seamless access and control over signage displays in public areas, streamlining the management process and enhancing advertising effectiveness.

QBSign Key Features

No Additional Player Needed

No additional player required for signage service with B2B signage display from Samsung and LG

Offline Content Storage

Content is stored locally in the display to ensure continuous service even if the network is disconnected.

Centralized Service Management

Centralized control over schedule and content display settings.

Content Scheduling

Schedule content with priority and calendar views.

Automation & OTT Support

Supports OTT content integration and automatic content delivery.
